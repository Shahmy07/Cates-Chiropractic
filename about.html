ABOUT THE DOCTOR

Dr. Cates grew up in the small town of Prairie Grove, Arkansas. He attained his bachelor’s degree in Science – Kinesiology, at Central Baptist College. There he was a part of a championship winning baseball team. He continued his education at the prestigious Cleveland University – Kansas City, where he received his Doctorate in Chiropractic (DC).

Dr. Cates is an avid outdoorsman and enjoys hunting, fishing, golfing, and men’s league baseball. He is married to the love of his life Katelyn and they have two very furry huskies, Kimber and Ruger.

Dr. Cates is passionate about helping people and is so grateful to be able to come back and serve the community he has grown up in.

We are committed to providing patients the care they need to achieve optimal wellness!
