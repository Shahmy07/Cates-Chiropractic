Complete family care

Cates Chiropractic is a chiropractic office in Northwest Arkansas dedicated to providing the best chiropractic care for your entire family, from infants and children all the way through to your golden years.

PREGNANCY & PEDIATRICS

Gentle prenatal chiropractic care can reduce the physical demands on both mom and baby throughout pregnancy and delivery. It is also important to have babies and children checked for spinal misalignments early and often as they reach important milestones. Pediatric chiropractic is our specialty!

FAMILY & WELLNESS CARE

From birth to adulthood, chiropractic care can help maintain proper body function, which in turn can lead to faster healing and improved quality of life. Most people have no idea how good their body can feel. Waking up with aches, pain, stiffness and fatigue is common but not normal. Chiropractic can help! Learn more about our services here.

ATHLETES

Ninety percent of world-class athletes use chiropractic care to prevent injuries and increase performance potential. In our office, we treat athletes of all ages and abilities, from casual golfers to professional dance team members, to help shorten recovery times, increase range of motion, increase flexibility, and lessen the chances of injury.

PERSONAL INJURY

It is important to be evaluated as soon as possible after an auto accident or personal injury. Seeking chiropractic care is your best bet for overcoming the pain you are experiencing while accelerating the healing process and preventing lasting issues. Many are surprised to learn chiropractic care is usually covered by insurance as part of a personal injury claim.
